About the Attorney

Raised on a Cattle Ranch.
Rooted in the Work.

Andrew R. Farwell grew up on his family's cattle ranch outside Kirksville, Missouri. He graduated from Kirksville High School, earned his bachelor's degree with honors from the University of Arkansas, and his law degree from the University of Missouri–Columbia in 2002.

In December 2003 — one year out of law school — he opened his own practice in Kirksville. He could have gone somewhere bigger. He stayed. He has been at the same address ever since.

In twenty-two years, Andrew has represented clients in more than thirty-five county courts across northern and central Missouri. He has argued before both Districts of the Missouri Court of Appeals, U.S. Federal District Courts, and litigated a six-week jury trial in California.

Andrew and his wife Allison came back to Kirksville to raise their children in the same community where he grew up. When he represents a client, he's representing a neighbor.
